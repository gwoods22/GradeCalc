Graeme's Grade Calculator

"The easiest not so easiest way to calculate your GPA"

Step 1:

Download the Extension .zip here then unzip it.

Step 2:

Open the Extension Dashboard by copying and pasting the follow link into your search box. Make sure you turn the Developer mode switch on, then drag the unzipped folder onto the window and come back here.

chrome://extensions

Step 3:

Go to Mosaic and log in then come back here.

Step 4:

Go to Grade Summary and then click the new books icon in the top bar to reveal your calculated GPA.
